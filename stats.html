The Statistics

All film Roles by Race (2017)

This chart shows that about 77% of film roles were white. This is disproportionate to the demographics of America which makes these statistics worrisome.

Cable Scripted Shows by Race (2016-2017 season)

This chart shows 71.8% of actors in scripted cable shows were white which is once again disproportionate to America's demographics at the time.

Percentage of shows directed by Minorities (2016-2017)

This shows less than 11% of the episodes were directed by minorities. This is even more staggering than the actor representation.

Minority Share of Writing Credits in Broadcast Scripted Shows (2016-2017)

This shows the majority of minority writers for broadcast scripted shows have written for roughly 11% of the episodes.
